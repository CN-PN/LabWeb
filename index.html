CNPN Lab

Clinical Neurophysiology & Precision Neuromodulation Lab

Our Vision & Mission

We integrate brain, cardiac, and autonomic physiology to decode, design, and deliver precision neuromodulation therapies, restoring dysfunctional brain-body networks in neurological, psychiatric, neuromuscular and neurocardiac disorders.

What We Do

We restore dysfunctional brain–body networks in neurological, psychiatric, neuromuscular and neurocardiac disorders through precision-guided interventions.

👉 WE ARE NEUROSTIMULATIONIST

A neurostimulationist is a clinician who uses advanced brain stimulation techniques to help manage neurological and mental health conditions. They carefully assess your symptoms and medical data to determine if this approach is right for you and to create a treatment plan that is personalized to your needs.
