Vision

We decode dysfunctional systems and build therapies around physiology.

We integrate brain, cardiac, and autonomic physiology to decode, design, and deliver precision neuromodulation therapies, restoring dysfunctional brain-body networks in neurological, psychiatric, neuromuscular, and neurocardiac disorders.

WE ARE NEUROSTIMULATIONIST

Our Vision

The lab sits at the intersection of neuroscience, clinical medicine, and computational innovation to design therapies that are precise, adaptive, and measurable.

How We Work

A simple pipeline from signal to treatment.

As neurostimulation specialists, we consult with patients, perform clinical testing, and combine MRI, EEG, ECG, and behavioral assessment to understand the physiological state of each patient.

Personalized neuromodulation protocol

We integrate multimodal data to understand the disrupted brain circuits, then develop individualized stimulation targets, duration, and non-invasive stimulation strategies, primarily using tDCS, tACS, TMS, and taVNS.

Design, personalize, optimize

What Makes Us Unique

Clinical care, research rigor, and patient-centered design.

Patient counseling and follow-up

Structured patient counseling ensures treatment rationale, expected outcomes, and therapy plans are discussed clearly, with systematic follow-up to track response, refine protocols, and optimize outcomes over time.

Continuous optimization

Research
