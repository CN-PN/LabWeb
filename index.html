⚡Personalized Neuromodulation

Transforming Brain–Body Health Through Precision Neuromodulation

We bring together neuroscience, cardiac physiology, and advanced neuromodulation to study and restore brain-body function. By combining Electroencephalography (EEG), Autonomic Function Testing, Functional Near-Infrared Spectroscopy (fNIRS), Functional Magnetic Resonance Imaging (fMRI), and behavioral assessments, we decode individual brain–body states and develop personalized neuromodulation therapies using Transcranial Direct Current Stimulation (tDCS), Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS), and Transcutaneous Auricular Vagus Nerve Stimulation (taVNS) to improve clinical outcomes.
